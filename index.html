Flappy Bird For The 3DS (+ Site News)

First of all, sorry for not posting in a while. This was because I had nothing to post about and I lost some of my interest in SnugglesStuff. To make up, I’ll give you one of the best articles I can!

What it is:

Someone made the game Flappy Bird on the 3DS/DSi. For some people “Flappy Bird” is an “odd shade of blue”; according to the creator, they didn’t set it up properly.

What is Flappy Bird?

Flappy Bird is an (extremely annoying) game. To play you tap the screen to jump through pipes. If you touch a pipe you lose. You get one point for every pipe you pass through.

The controls:

The controls are pretty good. You either tap the screen or press A to jump.

How it works:

It uses an app called “Petit Computer” by SmileBoom to run. Petit Computer allows you to run and write programs in the language “SmileBasic”.

How to get it?

  1. Go here (http://img2.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20140212022026/petitcomputer/images/thumb/b/b3/Flappy_Bird_QR.png/512px-Flappy_Bird_QR.png) on your computer.
  2. Open Petit Computer on your 3DS/DSi.
  3. Tap “File Management”.
  4. Tap “Read QR Code”.
  5. Line the red box up with the boxes until you have scanned every one.
  6. Tap “Back” if it is on-screen.
  7. Tap “View gallery”.
  8. Tap “FLAPBIRD”.

How to run it:

  1. Tap “Back” if it is on-screen.
  2. Tap “View gallery”.
  3. Tap “FLAPBIRD”.
