Aero Glass in Windows 8.1

Aero Glass in Windows 8.1. It’s right here. And there is finally an installer. It’s a small detail but I miss it. A lot. (If you are wondering what Aero Glass is, it is some transparency on window borders.)

OK. So here is before:

 

And here is how to do it:

NOTE: You need 7-zip or winRAR for this tutorial.

  1. Right click on your Start button.
  2. Click System.
  3. Look under the category labeled System.
  4. Look at System type.
  5. If it says “64-bit Operating System” then remember 64-bit otherwise remember 32-bit.
  6. Close the window.
  7. Go Here.
  8. Look under Aero Glass for Win8.1 RTM Pre-release.
  9. Click 32-bit Windows or 64-bit Windows depending on what you remember.
  10. Wait five seconds.
  11. Open the file.
  12. Extract it to “C:\AeroGlass”.
  13. Open “C:\AeroGlass”.
  14. Run “install.bat” as administrator.
  15. Reboot.
  16. Wait.
  17. Click “Yes”.
  18. See your Aero Glass in Windows 8.1!
    After you do this:

So… There you go. Aero Glass in Windows 8.

UPDATE: Sometimes it does not work but this is rare.
